Luxa offers a carefully curated colour palette for modern interfaces: **Neutrals** and **Secondary Colours** optimized for both _sRGB_ and _P3_ colour spaces.

## Neutrals

Neutrals provide utility and hierarchy without competing with our core and secondary colours. Now, in _P3_, on supported displays for better display accuracy.
